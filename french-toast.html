French Toast

  1. Home
  2. French Toast

Ingredients

  • Bread: 4-6 slices (brioche, challah, or thick-cut white bread works best)
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Milk: 1/2 cup (whole milk, but you can also use half-and-half or cream for a richer flavor)
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Ground Cinnamon: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon (optional, for a slightly sweetened version)
  • Salt: A pinch
  • Butter: 2 tablespoons (for frying)
  • Toppings: Maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh berries, whipped cream, or your favorite toppings

Instructions

  • Whisk the eggs: In a shallow dish or a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, sugar (if using), and a pinch of salt until well combined.
  • Dip the bread: Heat a nonstick skillet or griddle over medium heat. While it’s heating, dip a slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly. Let it soak for a few seconds so the bread absorbs the mixture, but don’t let it get too soggy.
  • Repeat with all slices: Soak each slice of bread in the egg mixture.
  • Melt the butter: Once the skillet is hot, add 1 tablespoon of butter and let it melt, spreading it around to coat the surface.
  • Cook the bread: Place the soaked bread slices in the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and slightly crisp on the edges. You may need to cook in batches, depending on the size of your skillet.
  • Add more butter as needed: Add more butter to the skillet as needed when cooking additional batches.
  • Serve hot: Serve the French Toast hot, topped with your choice of maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh berries, whipped cream, or other favorite toppings.

Pumpkin Pie

  1. Home
  2. Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust:

  • All-Purpose Flour: 1 1/4 cups
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Unsalted Butter: 1/2 cup (1 stick, chilled and cut into small cubes)
  • Ice Water: 3-4 tablespoons

For the Pumpkin Filling:

  • Pumpkin Puree: 2 cups (either canned or homemade)
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Brown Sugar: 3/4 cup (packed)
  • Granulated Sugar: 1/4 cup
  • Ground Cinnamon: 1 teaspoon
  • Ground Ginger: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Ground Nutmeg: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Ground Cloves: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Evaporated Milk: 1 1/4 cups (or heavy cream for a richer pie)
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  • Mix the dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
  • Cut in the butter: Add the chilled butter cubes to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs, with some pea-sized pieces of butter remaining.
  • Add the ice water: Sprinkle the ice water over the flour mixture, one tablespoon at a time, mixing with a fork until the dough begins to come together. Add just enough water so that the dough holds together when you press it.
  • Form the dough: Gather the dough into a ball, flatten it into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or up to 2 days).
  • Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Roll the dough: On a lightly floured surface, roll the chilled dough into a 12-inch circle. Carefully transfer the dough to a 9-inch pie dish. Trim the excess dough, leaving about 1/2 inch overhang, and fold the edges under. Crimp the edges with your fingers or a fork.
  • Blind bake the crust: Line the pie crust with parchment paper and fill it with pie weights or dried beans. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment and weights, and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the crust is lightly golden. Set aside to cool.
  • Mix the filling: In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, eggs, brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, salt, evaporated milk (or cream), and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  • Pour the filling: Pour the pumpkin filling into the pre-baked pie crust.
  • Bake the pie: Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 50-60 minutes, or until the filling is set but still slightly jiggly in the center. A knife inserted near the center should come out clean.
  • Cool the pie: Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. The filling will continue to set as it cools.
  • Serve chilled or at room temperature: Pumpkin pie is traditionally served chilled or at room temperature. Top with whipped cream before serving, if desired.
